2. Points to Consider


Online & Offline Handwriting

There is a difference between Online and Offline handwriting. Our model was built for real-life handwritten texts. Testing images were obtained from an online handwritten image, negatively affecting the recognition accuracy.

BestPath Decoder & Model WordBeam Decoder

Unexpectedly, two of our decoders returned the same recognition results: BestPath and WordBeam. We believe this was because the trained model did not have enough word samples to create an optimized word beam model. Therefore, CTC word beam model we obtained online gave us a higher recognition accuracy.


3. Going Forward


Increasing Utility & Building Smarter Model

We eventually want to provide value by allowing a search functionality for handwritten notes. A few challenges ahead are: segmenting notes to our desired granularity, increasing recognition accuracy, and speeding up the recognition process.

Also, we want to improve the model accuracy by asking the users to correct the words that are inaccurately recognized. It would be ideal to create a system in which users benefit from the convenience of the model, and they also contribute to the model by helping us build a larger dataset of handwritten texts.
